Last updated: April 30, 2026

Art Clock Collection is designed as a quiet, finished art clock collection. The app does not require an account and does not send personal data to a developer-operated server.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect personal data from the app for developer use.

The app stores simple preferences on your device, such as selected scenes, display settings, widget scene selection, and unlock status. These preferences stay on your device or in the app group shared with the iOS widget.

## In-App Purchase

Art Clock Collection offers one non-consumable in-app purchase to unlock the full collection.

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment details are entered outside the app, and the developer does not receive or store your payment information.

## Widgets

If you set a widget scene, the app saves the selected scene name to the local iOS app group so the widget can display the chosen artwork. This information is used only on your device for widget functionality.

## Support

If you contact support through the public support page, your request is handled outside the app through GitHub. Do not include passwords, payment details, recovery codes, or other sensitive personal information in support requests.

Support page:

[https://concept-journey.github.io/art-clock/support/](https://concept-journey.github.io/art-clock/support/)

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the app's data practices change.
