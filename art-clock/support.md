Art Clock Collection is a quiet iOS clock made from 24 hand-drawn scenes: cats, night moods, and quiet reading.

## Get Help

For support requests, open an issue on GitHub:

[Create a support request](https://github.com/concept-journey/concept-journey.github.io/issues/new?title=Art%20Clock%20Collection%20Support)

Please include:

- your iPhone model
- your iOS version
- the app version
- what you expected to happen
- what happened instead

Do not include Apple ID passwords, payment details, recovery codes, or other sensitive personal information.

## Purchases And Restore

Art Clock Collection uses one non-consumable in-app purchase to unlock the full collection.

If you already purchased the unlock, use the app's restore purchase option. Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store.

## App Scope

The app is a finished collection:

- 24 hand-drawn clock scenes
- 3 collections
- first scenes available as free previews
- one-time unlock for the full collection
- no subscription
